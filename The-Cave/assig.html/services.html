Complaints

We strive to provide an exceptional dining experience for all our guests.
If you have any concerns or complaints during your visit,
please notify our staff immediately so we can address and resolve the issue promptly.
We value your feedback and aim to improve our services continuously.

Price and Payment

All prices listed on our menu are inclusive of applicable taxes and are subject to change without prior notice.
Payments can be made via cash, credit card, or debit card. Please note that we do not accept checks.
All payments must be settled in full before leaving the premises.
In the event of a billing dispute, please bring it to the attention of the management at the time of payment for immediate resolution.

Cancellation and Limitation of Liability

Reservations can be canceled or modified up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any penalty.
Cancellations made within 24 hours of the reservation time may incur a cancellation fee.
We understand that plans can change, and we appreciate your cooperation in informing us promptly.
By dining at our restaurant, you agree that our liability is limited to the cost of your meal,
and we are not responsible for any indirect or consequential damages
